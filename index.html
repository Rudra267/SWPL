about us

Building quality through industrial innovation

▪️ The journey of Surana Wires Private Limited started in 1974 in Hyderabad, India. We have been offering the best in wire and fencing products for over 40 years.

▪️ India’s leading manufacturer of high-quality galvanized and welded wire mesh. Producing over 26000 tons per annum of galvanized wire and welded wire mesh.

▪️ We operate in two locations: Nacharam, Rangareddy District Chegunta, Medak District.
